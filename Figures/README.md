# 🖼️ Project Figures & Conceptual Models
‌
This folder contains the visual frameworks for the **Decolonial AI-mediated Writing (DAR)** project. These models illustrate the transition from passive AI reliance to critical epistemic agency.
‌
---
‌
### 1. The DAR Process Framework
**File:** `806848e8-2f75-4f9a-91fd-46e9bc10e2a9.png` (or renamed to `DAR-Framework.png`)
‌
- **Description:** This figure illustrates the three-level transformation (Cognitive, Discursive, and Interactional) facilitated by the DAR pedagogical intervention. It shows how learners move from "Epistemic Submission" to "Epistemic Awareness."
‌
---
‌
### 2. The Structural Dimensions of DAR
**File:** `IMG_20260606_071233.jpg` (or renamed to `DAR-Structural-Model.jpg`)
‌
- **Description:** A concentric model showcasing the nested nature of the DAR framework. It highlights the integration of sociocultural contexts, power equity, and authorial voice reclamation within the AI-mediated writing process.
‌
