# Research Instruments: DAR Framework
‌
This folder contains the instruments used to measure the transition from Epistemic Submission to Negotiated Agency.
‌
---
‌
## 1. Epistemic Agency Checklist (EAC)
Goal: To measure the student's critical control over AI during the writing process.
‌
Rate each item on a scale of 1 (Never) to 5 (Always):
‌

Questioning Authority: Did the student challenge an AI suggestion that seemed to "flatten" their original meaning?
Strategic Prompting: Did the student use "Resisting Prompts" to set boundaries for the AI?
Rationale Provision: Can the student provide a pedagogical or rhetorical reason for accepting/rejecting an AI edit?
Source Verification: Did the student verify the epistemic accuracy of AI-generated claims?
Boundary Setting: Did the student explicitly instruct the AI to preserve specific cultural or personal metaphors?
‌
---
‌
## 2. Authorial Voice Rubric (AVR)
Goal: To assess the presence of the writer's unique identity in AI-mediated texts.
‌
| Level | Criteria | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Level 4: Agentic | Strong Presence | The text reflects a clear personal stance; cultural nuances are preserved; AI is used only for polishing. |
| Level 3: Negotiated | Emergent Voice | The writer successfully defends their main arguments against AI flattening, though some generic AI phrasing remains. |
| Level 2: Mimetic | Weak Presence | The text follows standard AI-generated patterns; original metaphors are replaced by generic academic clichés. |
| Level 1: Submissive | Erased Voice | The writer accepts all AI changes without scrutiny; the authorial "I" is lost in algorithmic neutrality. |
‌
---
‌
## 3. Qualitative Interview Protocol (Post-Intervention)
How did your perception of the AI change after using the "Resisting Prompts"?
Can you describe a specific moment where you felt the AI was "erasing" your cultural identity?
How do you decide which AI suggestions to reject?
‌
---
Note: These instruments were validated for the study: "Decolonizing the Algorithm" (Merrikhi, 2026).
