
# Statistical Summary (L2 Writing Data)
‌
Since the raw participant data is protected under ethical privacy agreements, this document provides the aggregated statistical summary used for the final analysis.
‌
### Descriptive Statistics (N=30)
- **Pre-Intervention Mean (EA):** 2.45 (SD: 0.65)
- **Post-Intervention Mean (EA):** 4.18 (SD: 0.42)
- **Confidence Interval:** 95%
‌
### Data Processing Notes
- All data was normality-tested using Shapiro-Wilk.
- Non-parametric Wilcoxon Signed-Rank tests were chosen due to the ordinal nature of the rubrics.
- Statistical software used: SPSS v.26.
